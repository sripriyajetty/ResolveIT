# ResolveIT - Women Harassment Grievance and feedback management system

The Smart Women Harassment and Grievance System is a secure web-based system that aims to assist women in reporting harassment and grievances in a safe and efficient manner within an institution. The system is designed to promote confidentiality, transparency, and timely resolution of complaints through a systematic workflow process for complaint submission, investigation, and resolution.

The system is developed to handle three types of users: Users (victims), Committee Members, and Admin. Users are able to register, lodge grievances, check the status of their complaints, and give feedback. Committee Members are responsible for reviewing and analyzing complaints, updating the status of cases, and escalating complaints if they remain unresolved. Admins are responsible for managing users, allocating cases, tracking escalations, and keeping audit trails and reports to ensure accountability.

The Smart Women Harassment and Grievance System is an improvement over existing grievance systems as it offers a centralized, transparent, and efficient online solution that increases accessibility, prevents delays, and ensures proper complaint handling.
